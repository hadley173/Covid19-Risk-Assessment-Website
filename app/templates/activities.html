Activity risks

In July 2020, the Texas Medical Association (TMA) COVID-19 Task Force and the TMA Committee on Infectious Diseases created a chart to help people understand the relative risk levels of common activities during the pandemic. We used this chart as a basis for our activity risk ratings.

The relative risk ratings assume that people are following CDC guidelines on hygiene, personal protective equipment, and social distancing.
